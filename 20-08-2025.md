<b>2.Write a program to print diamond pattern.</b>

<b>code:</b>

n = int(input("Enter number of rows: "))

for i in range(n):

    print(" " * (n - i - 1) + "*" * (2 * i + 1))

for i in range(n - 2, -1, -1):

    print(" " * (n - i - 1) + "*" * (2 * i + 1))
